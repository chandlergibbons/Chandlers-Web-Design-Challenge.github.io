Summary: Latitude vx. X

The Purpose of this project was to analyze how cities weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To build this analysis I gathered raw data from the OpenWeatherMap API to gather weather data from over 500 cities across the globe. After gathering the data and cleaning , we utilized pythons Matplotlib liberary to plot diferent weather eliments against latitudinal plane. We looked at max tempature, cloudiness wind speed and humidity. This site will provide you with our data source as will as our findings and visualisations. It will also present explanations and descriptions of all trends and coorelations witnessed
